Discover Barnabas Aid Australia: Standing with the Persecuted Church

Standing with Our Persecuted Family

Barnabas Aid Australia stands with Christian brothers and sisters globally facing persecution. We are their voice, help, and hope, channeling support from Christians to Christians via trusted partners on the ground.

Our Purpose

Mission

To provide material and spiritual support, raise awareness, and advocate for suffering Christians.

Vision

A strengthened, encouraged persecuted Church maintaining its presence and witness, tackling injustice.

Values

  • Compassion (Galatians 6:10)
  • Prayer
  • Partnership with local leaders
  • Integrity (low overheads, transparency)

A Global Reach, A Local Heart

For years, Barnabas Aid has been a lifeline. We send aid, not people, empowering local Christians to build resilience through education, church rebuilding, and livelihood support, while sharing their untold stories and advocating for religious freedom.
